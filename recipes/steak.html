Steak

Description

A simple fried steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Peper
  • Garlic powder
  • Any type of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Let steak reach room temperature. Coat both sides in pepper, salt, and garlic powder.
  2. Heat pan with a layer of the oil until white smoke appears.
  3. Add steak to the pan and sear for 3-5 minutes per side until steak has reached desired doneness.
