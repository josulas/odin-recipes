Spaghetti

Description

This recipe is about how to cook spaghetti al dente.

Ingredients

  • 100 g of spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Optional: cream or butter

Steps

  1. Boil 1 L of water in a medium size pot. Before boiling, put salt as you like.
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook it for the time specified in the package.
  3. Drain the spaghetti. Optionally, you can put some butter or cream on it.
