Rice

Description

How to perfectly cook white rice.

Ingredients

  • White rice

Steps

  1. Rinse the rise with a fine mesh sieve.
  2. In an uncovered pot, add 1 cut of rice and 1 1/2 and bring to boil.
  3. Once boiling, cover it with a lit, put low heat and let it simmer for 15 min.
  4. Turn of the heat and let the rice fluff for 5 min.
